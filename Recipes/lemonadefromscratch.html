Lemonade from scratch

Description

This is an easy recipe for making lemonade from scratch (disclaimer, only for omnipotent beings)

Ingredients

  • Omnipotence.
  • Lemons.
  • Water (both liquid and solid).
  • Sugar.

Steps

  1. Create universe.
  2. Harvest lemons.
  3. Cut lemons and squeeze juice into liquid water.
  4. Add sugar to taste.
  5. Add solid water (also known as ice), or alternatively absorb kinectic energy from mix to desired temperature.
