Curry Chicken

Description

What better way to cook chicken than with the flavour

and spices that Indian cuisine gives us.

In this short recipe I will teach you how to bring the

taste of the East to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 800 grams of chicken breasts
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Red or Green pepper
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 1 can of coconut milk or cooking cream
  • Curry
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 25 grams of Cilantro
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop onions, garlic and peppers and stir fry in olive oil.
  2. Cut chicken in cubes and add to the mix, cook until white on the outside, season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Add coconut milk/cooking cream, mix with curry and stir in medium heat.
  4. Add chopped cilantro once chicken is cooked on the inside.
